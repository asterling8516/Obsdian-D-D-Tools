# Bane

**Bane**
_1st-level Enchantment_
**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Attack/Save:** Save
**Range:** 30 ft
**Components:** V S M (a drop of blood)
**Duration:** 1 minute
**Damage/Effect:** Effect
