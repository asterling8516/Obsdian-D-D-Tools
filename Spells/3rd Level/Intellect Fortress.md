# Intellect Fortress

**Intellect Fortress**
_3rd-level Abjuration_
**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Attack/Save:** Save
**Range:** 30 ft
**Components:** V
**Duration:** 1 hour
**Damage/Effect:** Damage
