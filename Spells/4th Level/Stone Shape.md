# Stone Shape

**Stone Shape**
_4th-level Transmutation_
**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Attack/Save:** None
**Range:** Touch
**Components:** V S M (soft clay)
**Duration:** Instantaneous
**Damage/Effect:** Effect

*You touch a stone object of Medium size or smaller or a section of stone no more than 5 feet in any dimension and form it into any shape you like. For example, you could shape a large rock into a weapon, statue, or coffer, or you could make a small passage through a wall that is 5 feet thick.
