# Heward's Handy Spice Pouch

**Heward's Handy Spice Pouch**
_Trinket_
**Type:** Trinket
**Quantity:** 1
**Weight:** N/A
**Price:** 30 gp
**Rarity:** Common
**Attunement:** None

*This belt pouch appears empty and has 10 charges. While holding the pouch, you can use an action to expend 1 of its charges, speak the name of any nonmagical food seasoning (such as salt, pepper, saffron, or cilantro), and remove a pinch of the desired seasoning from the pouch. A pinch is enough to season a single meal. The pouch regains 1d6 + 4 expended charges dail<span class="No-Break">y at dawn.</span>*
